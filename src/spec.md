# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create a 3D kart racing game inspired by Mario Kart with 10+ playable characters, power-ups, and physics-based racing mechanics.

**Planned changes:**
- Build a 3D racing track using Three.js/React Three Fiber with collision detection and physics
- Implement character selection screen with at least 10 unique characters with distinct kart designs
- Add Mario Kart-style racing mechanics: acceleration, braking, drifting/power sliding, and collectible power-up items
- Create race state management tracking lap count, race position, time, and start/finish conditions with countdown and results screen
- Store race completion records in backend for leaderboard tracking (character, time, track)
- Design vibrant cartoon aesthetic with bright colors, playful UI, and smooth animations

**User-visible outcome:** Players can select from 10+ unique characters, race on a 3D track with Mario Kart-style controls and power-ups, complete laps while being timed, and see their results saved to a leaderboard.
